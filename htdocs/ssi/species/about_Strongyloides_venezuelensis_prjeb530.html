Summary

The Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute (WTSI) assembly.

Assembly

The reference genome was produced by the Parasite Genomic group at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute in collaboration with the Division of Parasitology at the University of Miyazaki, as part of the WTSI Strongyloididae Nematode Genomes Project, (unpublished).

Annotation

The genome was annotated by the Parasite Genomics group at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute in collaboration with the Division of Parasitology at the University of Miyazaki, as part of the Strongyloididae Nematode Genomes Project (unpublished). An in-house automatic pipeline (based around MAKER) was used to generate first-pass gene models, and this was followed by a round of targeted manual curation of selected gene families.
