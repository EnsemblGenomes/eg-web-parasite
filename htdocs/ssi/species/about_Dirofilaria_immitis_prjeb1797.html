Edinburgh assembly.

Assembly

The D. immitis genome was sequenced by the Blaxter laboratory at University of Edinburgh. The data represented here is version nDi.2.2 from nematodes.org.

Annotation

The genome has been annotated by the Blaxter laboratory at the University of Edinburgh. The annotation version represented here is nDi.2.2.2 from nematodes.org.
