Summary

The Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute (WTSI) assembly.

Assembly

The H. contortus reference genome was sequenced by the Parasite Genomics group at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute, as described by Cotton et al (2013). The assembly version shown here is that submitted directly to WormBase in summer 2013.

Annotation

The genome was annotated by the Parasite Genomics group at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute, as described in Cotton et al (2013). The annotation version shown here is that submitted directly to WormBase in summer 2013.
