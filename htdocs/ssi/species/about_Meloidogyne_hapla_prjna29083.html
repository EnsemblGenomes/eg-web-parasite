Summary

North Carolina State University assembly.

Assembly

The M. hapla genome has been sequenced by the Plant Nematode Genomics group at North Carolina State University, as described by Opperman et al (2008).

Annotation

The genome was annotated by Plant Nematode Genomics group at North Carolina State University, as described by Opperman et al (2008). The annotation represented here is that submitted to WormBase in 2008.
