About Meloidogyne hapla

Root-knot nematodes (Meloidogyne spp.) are the most damaging and economically significant plant-parasitic nematodes worldwide. Meloidogyne hapla is a tractable model plant-parasitic nematode amenable to forward and reverse genetics.

Assembly

The Department of Energy Joint Genome Institute and at the Center for Biology of Nematode Parasitism produced six WGS libraries from M. hapla strain VW9 eggs on ABI 3730 and MegaBase sequencers. Trimmed sequences were assembled by using the Arachne package (Broad Institute) [PMID:19712469].
