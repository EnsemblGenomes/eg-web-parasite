Summary

The Genome Institute at Washington University (WUGI) assembly.

Assembly

The N. americanus genome was sequenced by the Mitreva laboratory at the Genome Institute of Washington University, as described by Tang et al (2014).

Annotation

The genome annotated by the Mitreva laboratory at the Genome Institute of Washington University, as described by Tang et al (2014). The annotation represented here was taken from the INSDC records.
