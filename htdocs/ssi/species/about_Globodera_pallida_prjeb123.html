Summary

The Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute (WTSI) assembly.

Assembly

The G. pallida genome was sequenced by the Parasite Genomics group at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute, as described in Cotton et al (2014). The assembly version represented here is that used in the analysis for publication. It is practically the same INSDC assembly GCA_000724045.1, with scaffold gap sizes being the only difference.

Annotation

The genome was annotated by the Parasite Genomics group at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute, as described in Cotton et al (2014).
