Summary

The Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute (WTSI) assembly.

Assembly

The T. muris reference genome has been sequenced by the Parasite Genomics group at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute, as described by Foth et al (2014). The assembly represented here is practically the same as NCBI assembly GCA_000612645.1, with scaffold gap sizes the only difference.

Annotation

The genome was annotated by the Parasite Genomics group at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute, as described by Foth et al (2014).
