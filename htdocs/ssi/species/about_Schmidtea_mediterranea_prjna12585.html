Summary

The Genome Institute at Washington University (WUGI) assembly.

Assembly

The S. mediterranea genome has been sequenced by the Stowers Institute and the Genome Institute at Washington University. The assembly version represented is SmedGD_v1.3 from SmedGD, as described by Robb et al (2007).

Annotation

The genome was initially annotated by the Stowers Institute, as described by Robb et al (2007). The annotation version represented here is v1.3 from SmedGD.
