Summary

The Genome Institute at Washington University (WUGI) assembly.

Assembly

The P. pacificus genome has been sequenced by the Sommer group at the Max-Planck Institute for Developmental Biology. The assembly version represented here (P_pacificus-v2) has been current in WormBase since 2010, and also browsable at pristionchus.org. This version has not been submitted to the INSDC.

Annotation

The genome was initially annotated by the Sommer group at the Max-Planck Institute for Developmental Biology. Some of the gene models have subsequently been revised by WormBase. The annotation version here is from WormBase WS243.
