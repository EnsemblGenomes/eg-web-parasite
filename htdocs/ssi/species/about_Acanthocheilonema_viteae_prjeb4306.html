Summary

Edinburgh assembly.

Assembly

The A. viteae genome has been sequenced by the Blaxter laboratory at University of Edinburgh. The assembly version represented here is nAv1.0, obtained from nematodes.org.

Annotation

The genome has been annotated by the Blaxter laboratory at the University of Edinburgh. The gene set version represented here is nAv.1.0.1 from nematodes.org.
