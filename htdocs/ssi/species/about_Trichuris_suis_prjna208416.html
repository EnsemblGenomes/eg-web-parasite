Summary

Melbourne assembly of the female T. suis isolate.

Assembly

The genome of a female T. suis isolate (strain DCEP-RM93F) was sequenced by Gasser laboratory at the University of Melbourne, as described by Jex et al (2014). The assembly version represented here was submitted directly to WormBase in early 2014.

Annotation

The genome was annotated by the Gasser laboratory at the University of Melbourne, as described by Jex et al (2014). The gene set represented here is that submitted directly to WormBase in early 2014.
