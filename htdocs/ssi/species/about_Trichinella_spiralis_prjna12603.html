Summary

The Genome Institute at Washington University (WUGI) assembly.

Assembly

The T. spiralis genome assembly was produced by the Mitreva group at the Genome Institute of Washington University, as described by Mitreva et al (2011).

Annotation

The genome was annotated by Mitreva group at the Genome Institute of Washington University, as described by Mitreva et al (2011). The annotation represented here was taken from the INSDC records.
