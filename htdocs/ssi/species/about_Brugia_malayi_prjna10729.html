Summary

WormBase assembly.

Assembly

The B.malayi genome was first sequenced at the former TIGR institute and described by Ghedin et al (2007). In 2012 a new genome sequencing, assembly and curation project was conducted on the FR3 strain as a cooperative effort between WormBase and the University of Pittsburgh. This sequenced FR3 strain is available at the Filiarisis Research Reagent Center.

Annotation

The gene models are actively curated by WormBase. The gene set version represented here is from WormBase release WS243.
