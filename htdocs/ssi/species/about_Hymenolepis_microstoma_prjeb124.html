Summary

The Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute (WTSI) assembly.

Assembly

The H. microstoma genome was sequenced by the Parasite Genomics group at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute, in collaboration with Pete Olson (Natural History Museum, London). The initial version of the genome was described in Tsai et al (2013). The assembly version presented here is the March 2014 snapshot from GeneDB. This is practically the same as INSDC assembly GCA_000469805.1, with scaffold gap-sizes being the only difference.

Annotation

The genome was annotated by the Parasite Genomics group at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute. The initial version of the annotation was described in Tsai et al (2013). The gene set version represented here is the March 2014 snapshot from GeneDB.
