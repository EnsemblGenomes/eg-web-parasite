Summary

Chinese Human Genome Center assembly.

Assembly

The S. japonicum genome was sequenced by a consortium led by the Chinese National Human Genome Center in Shanghai, and published in 2009. The assembly version represented here is the December 2013 snapshot, and and is practically the same as INSDC assembly GCA_000151775.1, with scaffold gap sizes the only difference.

Annotation

The genome was initially annotated by the Chinese National Human Genome Center, and published in 2009. The annotation has subsequently been improved, and the version represented here is the December 2013 snapshot from GeneDB.
