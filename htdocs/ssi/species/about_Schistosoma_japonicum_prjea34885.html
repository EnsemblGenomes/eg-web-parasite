Summary

Chinese Human Genome Center assembly.

Assembly

The S. japonicum genome was sequenced by the Schistosoma japonicum Genome Sequencing and Functional Analysis Consortium, and published in 2009. The assembly version represented here is the December 2013 snapshot, and and is practically the same as INSDC assembly GCA_000151775.1, with scaffold gap sizes the only difference.

Annotation

The genome was initially annotated by the Schistosoma japonicum Genome Sequencing and Functional Analysis Consortium, and published in 2009. The gene set version represented here is the December 2013 snapshot from GeneDB.
