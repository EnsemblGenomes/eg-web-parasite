Summary

The Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute (WTSI) assembly.

Assembly

The E. multilocularis reference genome was sequenced by the Parasite Genomics group at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute. The initial version of the genome was described in Tsai et al (2013). The assembly has subsequently been improved, and the version represented here is the December 2013 snapshot.

Annotation

The genome was initially annotated by the Parasite Genomics group at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute as described by Tsai et al (2013). The gene models have since undergone manual revision. The gene set version represented here is the December 2013 snapshot.
