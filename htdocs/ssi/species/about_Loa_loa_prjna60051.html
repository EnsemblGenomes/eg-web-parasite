Summary

Broad Institute assembly.

Assembly

The L. loa genome was sequenced by the Filarial Worms sequencing project at BROAD institute, as described by Desjardins et al (2013).

Annotation

The genome was annotated by the Filarial Worms sequencing project at BROAD institute, as described by Desjardins et al (2013). The annotation represented here was obtained from the INSDC records.
