Summary

University of Cologne assembly.

Assembly

The R. culicivorax genome has been sequenced by the University of Cologne, as described by Schiffer et al (2013). The data represented here is version nRc.2.0 from nematodes.org.

Annotation

The genome has been annotated by the University of Cologne, as described by Schiffer et al (2013). The gene set represented here is version nRc.2.0.1 from nematodes.org.
