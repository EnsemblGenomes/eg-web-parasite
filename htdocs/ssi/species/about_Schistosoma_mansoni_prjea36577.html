Summary

The Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute (WTSI)/JCVI assembly.

Assembly

The S. mansoni genome was sequenced by a consortium led by the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute, as described by Berriman et al (2009). The assembly version represented here is the v5.0 freeze from GeneDB (December 2013), and is practically the same as INSDC assembly GCA_000237925.2, with scaffold gap sizes the only difference.

Annotation

The genome was initially annotated by a consortium led by the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute, as described by Berriman et al (2009). The annotation version represented here is the v5.0 freeze from GeneDB (December 2013).
