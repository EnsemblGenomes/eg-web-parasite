We provide a number of ready-made tools for processing your data. At the moment, small datasets can be uploaded to our servers and processed online.

Currently available:

Tool Description    
Region Report Export standard data sets (genes, sequence, variations, etc) from one or more regions, in either GFF or simple text format Upload your data
(max 5MB total, or 1MB for variation/regulation data)
Variant Effect Predictor (Formerly SNP Effect Predictor). Upload a set of SNPs in our standard format and export a file containing consequence types. Uploaded tracks can also be viewed on Location pages. Upload your data
(max 750 variants)
Data Slicer Get a subset of data from a BAM or VCF file. Upload your data
(max 10K region)
